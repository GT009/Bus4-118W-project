ABOUT US

The Sandwich Factory is a restaurant located in San Jose that serves a wide variety of sandwiches. Our menu includes classic sandwiches such as The Club Sandwich and The Classic Sandwich, as well as unique creations like our signature Veggie Sandwich.


We believe that a great sandwich starts with great ingredients. That's why we use only the freshest and highest quality meats, cheeses, and vegetables in all of our sandwiches.


At The Sandwich Factory, we're passionate about providing our customers with delicious, satisfying meals that they can feel good about. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch or dining in with friends and family, we're committed to making your experience a memorable one.
