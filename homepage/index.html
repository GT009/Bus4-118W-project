About The Sandwich Factory

The Sandwich Factory was founded in 2023 by a group of likeminded and passionate students at San Jose State University. The founders had worked in the restaurant industry for years and noticed a gap in the market for a restaurant that specialized in high-quality sandwiches made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. They decided to open The Sandwich Factory in a small storefront in downtown, and their mission was simple: to make the best sandwiches in town using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. At The Sandwich Factory, we take pride in creating the most delicious and satisfying sandwiches that leave our customers feeling satisfied and happy.
